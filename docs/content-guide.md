# Content Guide

Good content sells sarees and helps Google find you. Keep it simple and
consistent.

## Product names

Format: **[Distinctive word] + [Weave/Fabric] + "Saree"**

- ✅ "Royal Gadwal Silk Saree"
- ✅ "Classic Kanjivaram Bridal Saree"
- ❌ "Saree 042" (not descriptive, bad for search)

## Product descriptions

Aim for 2–4 sentences. Cover:

1. **What it is** — the weave, fabric, and feel.
2. **What makes it special** — zari, motifs, origin, craftsmanship.
3. **When to wear it** — wedding, festive, daily, office.

> Example: "A handwoven Gadwal silk saree with a contrasting cotton body and a
> richly woven silk pallu. Crafted by master weavers in Gadwal, Telangana, with
> traditional temple motifs along the border. Perfect for weddings and festive
> occasions."

The first ~160 characters also become the Google/social preview text, so put
the most important words first.

## Highlights

Short, scannable selling points — **one per line** in the admin. 3–5 is ideal.

```
Pure silk pallu & border
Handwoven by master weavers
Comes with matching blouse piece
Traditional temple motifs
```

## Photos

- **Orientation:** portrait (tall), the way a saree drapes.
- **Size:** at least **1000 × 1500 px**. Bigger is fine — the site compresses
  automatically. This size is also ideal for Pinterest.
- **Lighting:** natural daylight, true-to-life colors. Customers buy on color.
- **Per color:** upload photos for *each* color variant so the gallery swaps
  correctly when a customer taps a swatch.
- **First photo = primary:** make the best, full-saree shot the primary image.

## Color names

Use clear, evocative names that match the swatch:

- ✅ "Maroon", "Peacock Blue", "Mustard Gold", "Emerald Green"
- ❌ "Color 1", "Dark"

## Collections

Collections group sarees around a theme or price point and are great to share
on Instagram/Pinterest. Examples already planned:

- Bridal Sarees, Wedding Sarees
- Gadwal / Kanjivaram / Banarasi / Pure Silk collections
- Sarees Under ₹5,000, Sarees Under ₹10,000

Price collections are **manually curated** — add qualifying sarees to the
collection on each product's edit page. Write a 1–2 sentence collection
description; it doubles as the page intro and the Google preview.

## Sharing for traffic

- Post sarees and collections to Instagram and Pinterest, linking back to the
  website.
- Add `?ref=instagram` or `?ref=pinterest` to shared links so the admin
  Inquiries/Subscribers pages show where each lead came from. Example:
  `https://yourdomain.com/collections/bridal-sarees?ref=instagram`
- Reply fast on WhatsApp — that's where sales happen.
